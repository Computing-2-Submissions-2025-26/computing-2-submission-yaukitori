How to play

Game modes

Click the mode button in the middle of the screen to cycle between modes: random → melody → perfect pitch → chord.

Random mode: a completely random sequence of 4 notes is chosen as the secret code each game.

Melody mode: the secret code is taken from semi-famous songs that bring back memories for me. Guess it to reveal what the song is and listen to it on YouTube!

Perfect pitch mode: a 6-note melody is played to you after a short "get ready…" countdown. Use the hear it again button (right of the mode button) to replay it as many times as you need, then try to reproduce all 6 notes exactly. 10 attempts.

Chord mode: a chord is played to you at the start. Your job is to identify every note in the chord — order doesn't matter. You have 5 tries. After each guess, circles glow green if the note is in the chord, or fade out if it isn't. Use the hear it again button (and the difficulty selector next to it) to control the challenge:
· Difficulty 1 — 3 white notes
· Difficulty 2 — 4 white notes
· Difficulty 3 — 4 notes including sharps (black keys)
· Difficulty 4 — 5 notes including sharps

  1. A secret code of piano notes has been chosen for you (either randomly generated, a melody, or in the form of chords).
  2. Click piano keys to build your guess. Notes play as you click.
  3. After each attempt, the 2×2 grid below the column will show whether you have guessed correctly. Each peg position matches the note of the same number:

Controls & tips

  • Keys a s d f g h j k play C D E F G A B C2 — the letter is shown on each white key.
  • In chord mode (difficulties 3 & 4), w e t y u also play C# D# F# G# A# — shown on the black keys.
  • Your guess is submitted automatically once you've picked enough notes.
  • Click a filled slot to remove that note from your guess.
  • Backspace or Delete removes the last note added.
  • The eye button (top right) swaps peg colours — helpful if red and green are hard to tell apart:
    • Blue: right note, right position.
    • Orange: right note, wrong position.
